Frequently Asked Questions

We know joining something new can feel intimidating, so here are answers to common questions:

Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners. Our community is all about learning and growing together. We'll provide workshops and mentorship to help you develop skills.

What if I'm not sure which team to join?

No problem! Many members participate in multiple teams. Come to the induction, talk to current members, and you can try different activities before deciding.

How much time commitment is expected?

We understand you have academic commitments. You can participate as much or as little as you want. We have weekly meetings but there's no mandatory attendance.

What's this about mindfulness and meditation?

We believe in nurturing both technical skills and mental well-being. We offer meditation sessions and mental health resources alongside our tech activities.
